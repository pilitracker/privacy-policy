Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 16, 2025

Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Pilik – Medication Tracker handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how your data is managed.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Pilik does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not require you to create an account, provide your name, email, or any personal details.

2. Local Data Storage

All medication schedules, reminders, and notes you create are stored locally on your device. Your data never leaves your phone and is not transmitted to any external servers or cloud services. You can remove all app data by deleting the application from your device.

3. No Internet Connection Required

Pilik is designed to work completely offline. It does not use the internet to sync, back up, or send any data to third parties.

4. No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use analytics tools, third-party SDKs, or tracking technologies of any kind. The app does not monitor user behavior, usage patterns, or device identifiers.

5. Notifications

Pilik uses local notifications to remind you to take your medications at the times you set. These reminders are created and managed solely on your device and never sent through external servers.

6. Permissions

The app may request limited permissions such as:

  • Notifications – to send local medication reminders.

These permissions are optional and fully controlled by you through your device settings.

7. Children’s Privacy

Pilik is suitable for users of all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal data from children or adults.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this Privacy Policy, they will be reflected in this document. We will not make changes that reduce your privacy protections without your consent.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, you can contact us at: pilik.medtracker@gmail.com

By using Pilik – Medication Tracker, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
